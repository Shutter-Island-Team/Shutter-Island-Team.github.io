Shutter Island

The procedural island generator with boids

The project

As you can see, the page is currently under development. You can check out the french web page in our school wiki if you are in a rush to understand what this project is all about.

Shaping the world

For each of the programm, a new island is procedurally generate.

Bringing life to the world

The island contains different type of species living, interacting, being born and dying. The boid logic combined with a graph deciding the state of the animal has been adopted for this project.

The boids here have the flocking behavior. It is currently running in real time on your machine!
